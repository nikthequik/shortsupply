Short Supply Wood Crafts is a seasonal wood shop creating functional one of a kind pieces from a variety of mediums. While specializing in wood turnings, Short Supply offers products from cement planters to mid-size furniture. Being located in Michigan's Upper Peninsula as I am gives me the unique ability to use many native wood species as the foundation of the majority of products crafted. Keeping in tune with the surrounding area, most items have a natural or nautical aesthetic to them.
Production runs from November through April, with pieces being sold throughout the year as my stock allows. Custom items are welcomed year-round as long as time restraints aren't required.
